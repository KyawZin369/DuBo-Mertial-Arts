FAQs

What types of classes does DoBu Martial Arts offer?
DoBu Martial Arts offers a variety of classes catering to different martial arts disciplines, fitness training, and self-defense courses. Whether you're interested in traditional martial arts like Karate and Taekwondo, or seeking fitness-oriented classes such as Cardio Kickboxing and HIIT workouts, we have something for everyone.
What facilities does DoBu Martial Arts provide?
Our martial arts gym boasts state-of-the-art facilities designed to enhance your training experience. We have a spacious, fully-matted martial arts area, a well-equipped gym with modern equipment, as well as additional amenities including a sauna and steam room for post-workout relaxation. Our changing and shower facilities ensure you can freshen up after your session.
How can I enroll in classes at DoBu Martial Arts?
Enrolling in classes at DoBu Martial Arts is quick and easy. You can either visit our gym in person to speak with one of our friendly staff members, or simply give us a call to discuss your preferences and schedule. We offer flexible membership options to suit your needs, whether you're looking for a single class pass or a monthly membership.
Are there any age or fitness level requirements to join DoBu Martial Arts?
No, there are no age restrictions or fitness level requirements to join DoBu Martial Arts. Our classes are designed to accommodate individuals of all ages and fitness levels, from beginners to advanced practitioners. Our experienced instructors will tailor the training to suit your capabilities and goals.
Can I try out a class before committing to a membership?
Yes, absolutely! We encourage all prospective members to try out a complimentary trial class to experience our training firsthand. This allows you to get a feel for our classes, meet our instructors, and see if DoBu Martial Arts is the right fit for you. Simply contact us to schedule your trial class today.
